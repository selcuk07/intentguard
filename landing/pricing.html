FAQ

What is an API request?

Each call to our verification, intent lookup, or analytics endpoints counts as one request. Protocol stats and pricing endpoints are free and don't count.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

The Free tier gives you full access to core verification features. Upgrade to Pro when you need higher rate limits, analytics, or webhooks.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your billing portal at any time. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

Do you accept crypto payments?

SOL and USDC payments are coming soon. For now, we accept all major credit cards via Stripe.

What about the on-chain protocol fee?

The on-chain verify_intent fee is separate from API pricing. It's currently set to 0 SOL and is governed by the protocol admin. API subscription covers hosted services (rate limits, analytics, webhooks).
